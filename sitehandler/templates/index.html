about us

we take care of your healthy life

We are a team of leading GPs and specialists who have come together to provide patient-centred, family-oriented medical services. Our team also includes nurse practitioners, nutritionists, physiotherapists and other healthcare staff so that you can treat many health concerns at a single destination.

Narail Sadar Hospital is founded to provide a comprehensive approach to diabetes management. Over time, we evolved into a trusted multi-speciality clinic focussed on Diabetes-Management, Family Medicine, Workplace Health and Health Education. Our doctor-led, patient-centric approach to disease management empowers and enables optimal health for you and your family, all under one roof.
